Extensions Built for Darras Hall Properties

Darras Hall is one of Northumberland's most prestigious residential estates, characterised by large detached homes set on generous plots with wide, tree-lined avenues. These properties have the space and the structure to benefit enormously from well-designed extensions — and the neighbourhood demands a standard of finish to match.

At C.A. Beresford, we have extensive experience working on properties across the Darras Hall estate. We understand the importance of matching existing brickwork, maintaining the proportions of the original build, and delivering a result that looks as though it was always there.

Many Darras Hall properties have large rear gardens with excellent potential for two-storey extensions — adding bedrooms above a kitchen or garden room below without losing significant outdoor space.

Common Questions

Answers to questions we often hear from Darras Hall homeowners

Do I need planning permission for an extension in Darras Hall?

Many single-storey rear extensions fall within Permitted Development rights, meaning no formal planning application is required. However, Darras Hall properties often have Article 4 directions or covenants that restrict certain changes. We'll advise you at the site visit and liaise with Northumberland County Council where needed.

How long does an extension typically take to build?

A single-storey extension typically takes 6–10 weeks once groundworks begin. A two-storey extension usually runs 12–18 weeks. These timescales depend on size and specification — we'll give you a clear programme at the quoting stage so you can plan accordingly.

Can you match the existing brickwork on my Darras Hall property?

Yes — matching brick is a priority for us on Darras Hall extensions. We source reclaimed and matching bricks to ensure the new work blends seamlessly with the original build. We'll assess the existing brick type during our site visit and identify the best match before any work starts.

Do you handle everything, including building regulations sign-off?

We manage everything from start to finish. This includes building regulations inspection bookings throughout the build and ensuring you receive your Completion Certificate at the end — essential for future property sales and mortgage queries.
