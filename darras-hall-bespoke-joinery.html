Artisan Joinery for Darras Hall's Premium Properties

Darras Hall is an estate defined by quality — and the joinery inside and around your home should reflect that standard. Whether you're fitting out a new extension, updating an ageing staircase, or adding bespoke storage to a loft conversion, the joinery we produce is made entirely by hand in our own workshop from premium timber.

Unlike off-the-shelf solutions, every piece we make is designed specifically for your home's dimensions, proportions, and style. We visit, we measure, we consult — and then we build something that fits perfectly and lasts a lifetime.

Many Darras Hall clients commission joinery as the finishing piece of a wider renovation or extension — the element that elevates a good build into something truly exceptional.

What We Make

Every item is handcrafted in our workshop and installed by our own team — no outsourcing, no compromise.

Staircases & Bannisters

A staircase is one of the first things you see on entering a home. Our bespoke staircases are engineered for safety and built for beauty — available in oak, pine, ash, and other hardwoods, with turned, square, or contemporary spindle profiles. We repair and refurbish existing staircases too.

  • Straight, quarter-turn, and spiral options
  • Oak, pine, ash, and hardwood choices
  • Replacement bannisters and spindles

Fitted Wardrobes & Storage

Made-to-measure fitted wardrobes that use every millimetre of available space — from floor to ceiling and wall to wall. We design around your storage needs and the room's architecture, including eaves spaces in loft conversions or alcoves in period rooms.

  • Full floor-to-ceiling designs
  • Alcove and eaves storage solutions
  • Sliding and hinged door options

Doors & Door Frames

Solid hardwood internal and external doors, made to size and specification. We craft traditional panel doors, contemporary flush designs, and glazed internal doors — all built to outlast off-the-shelf alternatives and suit the character of Darras Hall properties.

  • Internal and external hardwood doors
  • Glazed and panelled options
  • Bespoke frames and architraves

Gates, Fencing & Garden Structures

Darras Hall's wide driveways and generous front elevations benefit enormously from quality bespoke gates and fencing. We design and build hardwood entrance gates, side-access gates, post-and-rail fencing, and timber pergolas — all finished to complement your property's kerb appeal.

  • Bespoke entrance and side gates
  • Hardwood post-and-rail fencing
  • Pergolas and outdoor structures

Common Questions

Frequently asked by Darras Hall clients

Can you match existing joinery profiles in my Darras Hall home?

Yes — this is one of the most common requests we receive on Darras Hall properties. Whether you need to match existing skirting, architrave profiles, or staircase spindle styles, we can replicate them precisely in our workshop. We bring a sample from your property and match it exactly.

What timber do you use?

We work with a range of sustainably sourced timbers including oak, ash, pine, and sapele hardwood. For external items such as gates and fencing, we use treated hardwood species selected for durability in Northumberland's climate. We'll advise on the best choice for each application.

How long does bespoke joinery take to make?

Typical lead times run 3–6 weeks from order to installation, depending on complexity and our current schedule. Larger projects such as full staircases or a complete set of fitted wardrobes may take a little longer. We'll confirm a clear timeline in your quote.

Do you make joinery for new extensions and loft conversions?

Absolutely — we regularly combine joinery with our building work. If C.A. Beresford is building your extension or loft conversion, we can include bespoke staircases, fitted storage, and internal doors as part of the same project, saving time and ensuring everything integrates perfectly.
