Common Questions

Frequently asked by Darras Hall homeowners planning a renovation

Can we stay in the property during the renovation?

For partial renovations — such as a single kitchen or bathroom refit — most clients stay in the property throughout. For larger whole-house renovations, we'd recommend temporarily vacating if possible. We'll discuss this at the survey stage and plan the work sequence to minimise disruption.

Do you manage all trades or just the building work?

We coordinate the entire project. This includes our own building and joinery trades plus liaison with electricians and plumbers. You deal with us as a single point of contact — not a separate contractor for every trade.

How long does a kitchen renovation typically take?

A typical kitchen strip-out and full refit on a Darras Hall property takes 2–4 weeks depending on the scope of structural work. If wall removals or drainage reroutes are involved, we'll factor that into the programme at the quote stage.

Can you remove a load-bearing wall in my Darras Hall home?

Yes. We carry out structural alterations including load-bearing wall removals with RSJ steelwork. We work with a structural engineer to specify the correct beam and ensure building regulation compliance, including the necessary structural inspections.
