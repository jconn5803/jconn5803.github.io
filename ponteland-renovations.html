Renovating Your Ponteland Home

Ponteland is a sought-after area with a strong and resilient property market — and many of its homes were built in the 1960s, 70s, and 80s during a period when functional layouts mattered more than open-plan living. If you've bought an older property in the area, or have lived in one for years, a renovation can fundamentally transform how it feels to live there.

We work across the full spectrum — from a targeted kitchen and bathroom upgrade to a comprehensive whole-house refurbishment that includes structural alterations, replanning, new plastering, flooring, and all joinery. We manage all trades and coordinate every aspect of the build, so you have one clear point of contact throughout.

Renovating is nearly always more cost-effective than moving up the property ladder in Ponteland — and it means you can stay in the community, schools, and location you already love.

Common Questions

From Ponteland homeowners about renovation projects

Can we stay in the property during a renovation?

For partial renovations (kitchen, bathroom, or a single room), most Ponteland homeowners stay in place and we work room by room to minimise disruption. For a full whole-house renovation, we'd typically recommend alternative accommodation for the duration — we can advise on the most practical sequencing at the initial visit.

Do you manage all the trades or should I arrange them separately?

We manage and coordinate all the trades involved in your renovation — our role is to be your single point of contact throughout. This avoids the common pitfall of contractors waiting on each other and programme delays caused by poor sequencing.

How long does a kitchen and bathroom renovation typically take?

A combined kitchen and bathroom project typically takes 4–8 weeks depending on the existing layout, supply lead times for units and sanitaryware, and the extent of structural work required. We'll confirm your programme in writing before work starts.

Can you remove a load-bearing wall to open up the ground floor?

Yes — this is one of our most commonly requested works on Ponteland's older family homes. We obtain structural calculations, install the appropriate steelwork or beam, and ensure full building regulations compliance. We'll confirm at the initial survey whether any walls in your home are load-bearing.
