Common Questions

From Ponteland homeowners about general building work

Do you take on smaller jobs, or only large projects?

We take on jobs of all sizes in Ponteland — from repointing a garden wall or building a brick step, right through to a full extension or structural alteration. Our philosophy is that a small job done well is the best introduction to what we do. Many of our clients came to us originally with a modest repair and have since trusted us with major works.

Can you match the existing brickwork on my Ponteland property?

Matching brickwork is something we take seriously. Ponteland has a mix of brick types across different eras and developers, and a blatant mismatch on new brickwork is one of the most common complaints about building contractors. We take time to source appropriate materials and will discuss brick selection with you before any work starts.

Do you handle the building regulations approval for structural work?

Yes — for structural work such as wall removals, new lintels, and structural openings, we ensure the work is carried out to building regulations and that the appropriate sign-off (either full plans or building notice) is in place. We'll advise on what's required for your specific project at the initial visit.

Can you carry out work on a Ponteland property while it's tenanted or occupied?

For most general building work, yes — we work carefully around occupied properties and minimise disruption to occupants. We'll discuss specific access arrangements, working hours, and any necessary temporary protections at the planning stage so there are no surprises for you or your household.
