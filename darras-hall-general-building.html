Common Questions

Answers to questions from Darras Hall homeowners

Do you take on small jobs as well as large projects?

Yes — no job is too small for us. We carry out everything from single-day repairs and brick repointing through to months-long construction projects. Darras Hall homeowners often start with a smaller job and come back to us again when a larger project comes up — because they know the work will be done properly.

Can you match existing brickwork on my Darras Hall home?

Matching brickwork is something we take seriously, particularly on Darras Hall's premium properties. We source reclaimed or matching bricks and test them against your existing wall before committing — so new work blends in rather than standing out.

Are you fully insured?

Yes — C.A. Beresford carries full public liability insurance. We're happy to provide copies of our insurance documentation on request before any work begins.

Can you handle the structural side of a kitchen wall removal?

Yes — structural alterations including load-bearing wall removals and RSJ steel beam installation are within our scope. We work with structural engineers to ensure the correct specification and carry out the works to building regulation requirements.
