Roofing That Meets Darras Hall's High Standards

Darras Hall's large properties have substantial roofs — and the quality of your roof has a direct impact on both the integrity and the visual appeal of your home. Northumberland's climate demands robust, well-installed roofing that can handle driving rain, frost, and wind without fail.

We have over 25 years of roofing experience across Northumberland, and our team has worked extensively on Darras Hall properties — from full re-roofs in natural slate to leadwork repairs on flat roof sections and bay window canopies. We take pride in choosing the right materials and doing the job properly the first time.

Natural slate is an excellent roofing choice for Darras Hall's premium properties — durable, attractive, and well-suited to Northumberland's climate. We'll discuss the most appropriate material for your property and roof type.

Our Roofing Services

Comprehensive roofing for all types of Darras Hall properties — from full installations to targeted repairs.

New Roof Installation

Full new roof installations for extensions, new builds, and whole-roof replacements. We strip the existing structure, replace battens and felt, and lay new slates or tiles to a fully weathertight standard.

  • Natural slate
  • Concrete and clay tiles
  • New felt, batten, and counter-batten

Leadwork & Flashing

Specialist leadwork is critical at junctions between the roof and walls, dormer cheeks, valley gutters, and chimney flashings. Poorly executed leadwork is the most common source of roof leaks — we cut and dress lead correctly to ensure long-term performance.

  • Chimney flashing and soakers
  • Valley and parapet lead
  • Dormer cheek leadwork

Roof Repairs & Maintenance

Targeted repairs to lift and re-bed slipped or broken slates, repoint ridge tiles, clear and repair valley gutters, and reseal flashings. We provide an honest assessment of your roof's condition and only recommend work that is genuinely necessary.

  • Slipped and broken slate replacement
  • Ridge and hip tile re-bedding
  • Valley gutter clearance and repair

Flat Roofing

High-performance flat roofing for garages, extensions, and bay window canopies — using modern GRP (fibreglass) and EPDM rubber systems that offer long-term durability and a clean, professional finish.

  • GRP fibreglass roofing
  • EPDM rubber systems
  • Flat-to-pitched conversions

Roof Truss Manufacture & Installation

We manufacture bespoke roof trusses in our own workshop and install them on site for extensions and new builds. This means complete control over quality at every stage, and the ability to produce non-standard shapes that off-the-shelf suppliers won't accommodate.

  • Custom truss design and build
  • Attic and room-in-roof trusses
  • Structural timber frames

Guttering & Fascias

Replacement of failing gutters, downpipes, fascias, and soffits. We offer uPVC systems for low maintenance and cast-iron or aluminium ogee guttering for period properties and high-specification homes on the Darras Hall estate.

  • uPVC guttering and downpipes
  • Cast-iron and aluminium options
  • Fascia and soffit boarding

Common Questions

Frequently asked by Darras Hall homeowners about roofing

How do I know if my Darras Hall roof needs replacing or just repairing?

The honest answer depends on age, the extent of failure, and the underlying felt and batten condition. A roof that's losing a few slates is often repairable; a roof over 50 years old with degraded felt and widespread slate failure is usually more cost-effective to replace. We'll give you an honest assessment rather than defaulting to the bigger job.

What type of slate or tile do you use on Darras Hall properties?

We use natural slate for properties where quality and longevity are the priority — it's an excellent material for the Northumberland climate and looks well on Darras Hall's premium homes. Concrete and clay tiles are also used where appropriate. We'll discuss the best option for your property and give you our honest recommendation.

How long does a full re-roof take?

A full re-roof on a Darras Hall detached property typically takes 1–3 weeks depending on size, complexity, and weather. The property is never left exposed overnight — we ensure full weatherproofing at the end of each day.

Can you carry out roofing work as part of a loft conversion or extension?

Yes — roofing is an integral part of both our loft conversion and extension services. We manufacture our own roof trusses and carry out all roofing works in-house, so there's no need to manage a separate roofing contractor when we're already on site.
