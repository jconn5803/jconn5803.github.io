Ponteland is one of Northumberland's most desirable places to live, and its varied mix of family homes, period cottages, and larger detached properties all present excellent opportunities for well-designed extensions. Whether you're outgrowing your current footprint, want an open-plan kitchen-diner, or need additional bedroom capacity, an extension is often the most practical and cost-effective solution — especially given the premium that Ponteland property commands.

We work extensively across Ponteland and the surrounding villages of Medburn, Callerton, and Prestwick — and our team understands both the typical property types and the local planning authority's requirements for the area.

Extending in Ponteland rather than moving can save considerable cost in stamp duty, legal fees, and moving expenses — while staying in the community you already enjoy.

Common Questions

Frequently asked by Ponteland homeowners planning an extension

Do I need planning permission for an extension in Ponteland?

Many single-storey rear extensions fall under Permitted Development and don't require a formal planning application. However, two-storey and side extensions on semi-detached homes typically do require planning consent from Northumberland County Council. We'll advise clearly at the site visit stage.

Are there any restrictions in Ponteland I should know about?

Most of Ponteland is not within a conservation area, though some of the older properties closer to the village centre have additional planning considerations. Certain modern housing estates also have restrictive covenants. We'll identify any constraints relevant to your specific property before quoting.

How much does an extension typically cost?

Costs vary significantly depending on size, specification, and complexity — a modest single-storey extension will naturally cost less than a large two-storey addition. We provide detailed written quotes so you can plan accurately. Contact us for a free site visit and we'll give you realistic figures.

How long will my Ponteland extension take to build?

A single-storey rear extension in Ponteland typically takes 6–10 weeks from groundworks to completed internal fit-out. A two-storey extension runs 12–18 weeks. We confirm the programme in writing at the quoting stage.
